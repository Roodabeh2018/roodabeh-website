About

From engineering
to AI product thinking

I'm a Product Manager and UX Researcher with a background in Computer Science, Mechatronics Engineering, and Human–Computer Interaction.

My career started in engineering — fascinated by algorithms, robotics, and intelligent systems. Over time I became increasingly drawn to the people behind the technology, which led me into UX research and human-centered design.

Eventually I realized what excites me most is bringing people, technology, and business goals together to create products that solve meaningful problems. That's what led me into Product Management.

Today I work at the intersection of product strategy, user research, and emerging technologies — turning ambiguity into direction and helping multidisciplinary teams move from ideas to real-world impact.

A common thread throughout my work has been trust. Whether designing digital health solutions for older adults, researching security for vulnerable communities, or exploring AI-powered experiences, I keep returning to the same question: How can we build technology that people not only use, but genuinely trust?
